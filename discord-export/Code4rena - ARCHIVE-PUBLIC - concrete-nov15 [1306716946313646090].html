so, my understanding is that users get a better market rate by swapping their shares of X ConcreteVault for a rewardToken. Instead of withdrawing from the vault, they can get a better rate by swapping their shares for a rewardToken, and the vault's shares swapped by the user are sent to the Treasury. So, for users, opt to swap instead of withdrawing from the vault. For the protocol: attracts liquidity to the vaults (because first and foremost, to swap is required to have shares of a vault), and it mitigates withdrawals from the vaults, the already deposited assets are left on the strategies generating yield.
